WHERE THE IDEA CAME FROM?

It started when we joined a video game competion that took place from 7.May to 15.May. The assignment was a "World turned upside down." and the idea of the game was here.

After some time struggles, because it was during the week when we all had a school, we managed to finish the game and even won the competion.

  • this version is now for download at the website of the organizer - https://www.futurify.cz/project/gamejam-be/

WHERE WE ARE NOW

After the competion we leaved the game alone and had some break. After one year no new competion was in sight and we wanted to create a game. So we decided to make a full valuable game and rather then starting from a scratch, we decided to make the game from what we had from the competion.

We started on 2. of December 2022 with one new member joining in and one more on the spring. We have no date of release and still a lot of work to do, but we want to finish the game no matter how much time it takes.
