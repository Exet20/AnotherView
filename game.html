OVERALL

AnotherView is an Android mobile video game in development with minimalistic graphic that bets on particles and neon colors.

You control your player, who we like to call simply just "bean", and your goal is to progress through the levels by moving the player or changing gravity, which is the main aspect of the game. Of course, there are some obstacles in each level, and as you get further in the game, the levels geting bigger and more and new obstacles are appering.

GAME MECHANIC



PLAYER

The game mechanic for the player is very simple and easy to understand, so anybody can play the game.

Movement

  • move left - tap or hold the left side of the screen
  • move right - tap or hold the right side of the screen

Gravity

  • swipe left - turn gravity for 90°
  • swipe right - turn gravity for -90°
  • swipe up - turn gravity for 180°

Other

  • zoom - hold both left and right side of the screen

LEVELS

Your main concern is to get from the entry portal to the exit one, or siply the other than the one you came from.

As said before, there are some obstacles that you can encounter, for example spikes or lasers.

LEVEL SYSTEM

There are 6 chapters, each of them contains 8 levels. That leaves us with 48 of them, when not including 5 levels from the original version of the game + special chapters that may come after release of the game.

SKIN SYSTEM

The game include 6 different collors for the player and the 3 faces to make him look even more cooler.
